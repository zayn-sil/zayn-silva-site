About

I help people find language for the truths institutions avoid.

Zayn Silva is a speaker, preacher, and communications strategist working at the intersection of faith, identity, and justice.

As a transgender Latinx and Black man in ministry and public life, Zayn brings lived experience, spiritual depth, and strategic clarity to rooms navigating complex conversations in real time.

He has spoken at the United Nations, national conferences, and independent schools across the Northeast.

Signature Talks

Four conversations I'm bringing to rooms.

Adaptable for keynotes, assemblies, sermons, and breakout sessions.

Every talk is designed to give audiences language, clarity, and the courage to engage conversations they were avoiding before.

Faith & Identity

Queer and Called: Reclaiming Faith in a World That Tried to Erase You

A powerful and deeply personal talk on reclaiming faith, identity, and belonging in spaces that were never built to hold you. This talk challenges assumptions, invites healing, and gives audiences language for conversations they've been avoiding.

Best for: Churches, conferences, Pride events
Audience leaves with:
Language for faith and identity
Healing from harmful theology
Confidence to take up sacred space
Schools & Youth

You Belong Here: Identity, Courage, and Showing Up Fully

An empowering talk that meets students where they are, helping them build confidence, find language for who they are, and walk into the rest of their lives unafraid to take up space.

Best for: K–12, colleges, youth programs
Audience leaves with:
Confidence in their own identity
Tools to navigate hard rooms
A real sense of belonging
Corporate & DEI

Beyond Diversity: Building Cultures of Belonging That Actually Work

An honest, practical talk for leadership teams ready to move past performative inclusion. This talk names the failure modes most organizations don't want to admit, and gives leaders the language and frameworks to build cultures where people actually stay.

Best for: Companies, leadership teams
Audience leaves with:
Honest language for inclusion
Frameworks for real belonging
A clearer path forward as leaders
Parents & Families

When Your Child Comes Out: Faith, Fear, and Fierce Love

A compassionate, deeply honest conversation for parents and families navigating what it means to fully love a queer or transgender child. This talk creates space for fear, faith, grief, and growth, and sends parents home with real tools and a path forward.

Best for: Parent groups, churches
Audience leaves with:
Language for hard family conversations
Permission to feel and to learn
A path toward fierce, full-hearted love
